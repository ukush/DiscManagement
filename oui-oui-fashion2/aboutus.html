About Us

Founded in 2017

Two Entrepreneurs, Sam from Mauritius and Rafal from Poland founded Oui Oui Fashion. Oui Fashion brings unique fashion and latest trends from Europe to Mauritius. Having over 31000 followers on Facebook and over 11000 followers on Instagram Oui Oui Fashion has a well established on-line presence. Our mission is to deliver an outstanding customer service and quality fashion in Mauritius.
